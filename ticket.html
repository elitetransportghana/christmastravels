Terms and Conditions

Boarding and Verification

  • This receipt is a valid travel confirmation only for the named passenger and assigned seat shown above.
  • Elite Transport staff may request a matching phone number or ID and may scan the ticket barcode to confirm the booking before boarding.
  • Please arrive at the terminal at least 30 minutes before departure so verification and baggage checks can be completed on time.

Tickets, Changes and Refunds

  • Tickets paid through Paystack are verified before a booking is marked confirmed and released as a downloadable receipt.
  • Date, route or passenger-detail corrections should be requested before departure. Approval depends on seat availability and fraud checks.
  • Missed departures, invalid phone numbers or duplicate payment disputes may require manual review before changes or refunds are approved.

Baggage and Safety

  • Carry only lawful personal luggage. Oversized or commercial cargo may require separate approval from terminal staff.
  • Passengers must follow crew safety instructions during loading, boarding, transit stops and final arrival.
  • Elite Transport may refuse travel where a ticket appears altered, duplicated, transferred or fails barcode confirmation.
